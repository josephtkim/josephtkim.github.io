About
Hi there, thanks for stopping by! My name is Joseph Taewoo Kim, and this is a brief background about myself. As a child, I've always had a proclivity for mathematics. I won a gold medal in my gifted elementary school math competition, and obtained a perfect Math score on my SAT test. I ended up majoring in Mathematics at Penn State University, where I obtained my Bachelor of Science degree. Following my graduation, I moved to California. I originally came to visit my family for a vacation, but ended up staying here ever since. I fell in love with the weather and also felt that if I wanted to succeed in something, this was the place to do it. I started to teach myself programming a few months after landing in California. After learning for several months, including doing a challenge of making 30 coding projects in 30 days, I joined Codecademy as an educator. I taught students all over the world about programming concepts, and eventually had opportunities to author content on the platform. Following my time with Codecademy, I started my Software Engineering career at Google. There I learned a lot about software development, and also gained a strong interest in Machine Learning and AI. I worked on a project related to ML model migrations, attnended paper readings, and received a one-on-one monthly mentorship from a Staff Machine Learning Software Engineer. I also started reading any materials I could find on the subject and coded up several projects. After my time at Google, I took some personal time to travel, spend time with family and to continue studying Machine Learning. Currently, I am working as an AI trainer, to help train large language models, and as a mentor and tester on DeepLearning.AI. I'm interested in eventually getting a role that is in Software Engineering with a focus on ML/AI.

Outside of work, I enjoy reading, running, exercising, cooking, and learning new things (especially relating to ML/AI).
