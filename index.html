About
Hello there! I'm Joseph Kim. I have over 5 years of experience in tech; over 4 years spent in edtech and 1.5 years as a Software Engineer. I have a strong passion for Machine Learning and AI and looking for an opportunity to break into the field.

I studied Mathematics and Physics at the Pennsylvania State University, and after graduating I moved to California to visit family, and have been here ever since.

After moving to California, I taught myself programming for several months, and then joined Codecademy, where I authored content on the platform and helped people from around the world learn how to code in live online chat sessions.

Following my time at Codecademy, I started my Software Engineering career at Google, where I worked on the Google Ads High-Touch Support team. During that time, I gained a strong interest in Machine Learning and AI. I was involved with ML teams and attended paper readings, received one-on-one monthly mentorship from a Staff Machine Learning Software Engineer, and studied Machine Learning theory.

Outside of work, I enjoy reading, running, exercising, cooking, and learning new things.
